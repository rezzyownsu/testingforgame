Ragdoll Archers

Ragdoll Archers is a shooting game with ragdoll-like stick figures that can shoot arrows. Shoot arrows at enemies from a distance and collect points to improve your skills and weapons. You and a friend can play two-player PvP against each other or together to beat a variety of enemies.

How to Play

Watch how you line up your shots, and then fire!

You will start with basic shots and stats, and then you will face different enemies. Because of their protection, situation, and other things, some of these enemies are harder to beat than others. There will be times when you have to fight a huge enemy. You’ll get a lot of heads for beating these little bad guys, which is kind of like a boss level.

Stay alive as long as you can.

Your goal is to get as many heads as you can and stay alive as long as possible. Sticking to the apples is the best way to stay alive for a long time. You will see apples flying around during the game. To get more health, energy, or both, shoot your shot through the apples. To use the apples, do the following:

  • Red apple = health
  • Green apple = stamina
  • Golden apple = Health and stamina

    Change how your ammo works

    When you play the game more, you can unlock more and more arrow type. As you play, switch between different types of arrows to find the ones that work best for you and your way of play. You can add more arrow spots to hold more of them at once. The weapons range from electric and poison to balloon and axe.

    You can improve your skills by using your heads.

    When you kill an enemy, you get skulls. The number of skulls you get depends on how strong the enemy was. This coin can be used to improve your strength, energy, damage, and other things.
