What's Cookie Clicker Pro?

Cookie Clicker Pro is an idle clicker game where your goal is to bake as many cookies as possible. By clicking on a large cookie icon on the screen, you produce cookies. These cookies then serve as your currency to purchase various upgrades and buildings that automate cookie production, increasing your cookie output without continuous clicking.
