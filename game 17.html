The chases never cease to be exciting! Smashy Road is an action game that puts players in a race to escape the clutches of the law. The game presents a big challenge before the player: to be agile and clever enough to drive through busy streets, avoiding obstacles and outsmarting the relentless police force at their heels. During the process, Smashy Road constantly introduces new chases where you will unlock awesome cars and push your driving skills to the limit. Are you up for this unprecedented race?

Smashy Road Gameplay

In Smashy Road, you are controlling your vehicle to run as far as possible from the police. While driving, different obstacles stand in your way, causing you to easily get caught by the police. The longer you go without being taken into custody, the more complicated the chase: the number of police cars increases, and even helicopters and tanks join the action.

Collecting cash during the chase is needed, which can be used to unlock new vehicles. Each of the vehicles has different attributes that affect how they will handle during the game. Smashy Road is said to be easy to pick up, with simple touch controls for steering, and the fun lies in the chaotic chases.

Players Reviews on Smashy Road

Players who have experienced Smashy Road tend to praise the game for its addictive and simple gameplay. They mentioned how simple it is to pick up and play in short bursts. On the popular gaming community TouchArcade, users praised the variety of unlockable cars and chaotic yet satisfying gameplay, even describing it as "endless fun." On LevelWinner, players highly emphasize the unprediction in the game as one of its greatest charms, since it is challenging to survive for extended periods due to aggressive police tactics. Overall, it is recommended as a free game with entertaining and bite-sized action, without demanding a great amount of time.

FAQs

What are the types of vehicles available in the Smashy Road game?

Vehicles can be divided into Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary categories. Each one of these types of vehicles will possess certain specific attributes, such as speed, durability, and handling, that will definitely determine their performance during chases.

What if my wanted level increases?

As your wanted level increases, so does the aggression of the police units that are on your tail: armored vehicles, helicopters, and tanks. The longer you survive, the bigger the challenge.

What are the best strategies to survive longer on Smashy Road?

Players claim that good hints are trying to stay on open roads, using side streets to avoid police cars, and outrunning them by making sharp turns or using ramps.
